# Privacy Policy for Volume Boost Pro

**Last Updated: January 29, 2026**

## Overview

Volume Boost Pro ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome extension handles your information.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

Volume Boost Pro operates entirely locally on your device. The extension:

- ✅ Does NOT collect personal information
- ✅ Does NOT track your browsing history
- ✅ Does NOT store or transmit audio content
- ✅ Does NOT use analytics or tracking services
- ✅ Does NOT require user accounts or registration
- ✅ Does NOT display advertisements

## Local Storage

Volume Boost Pro uses Chrome's local storage API solely to:

- Save your volume preferences for individual websites
- Remember your selected audio preset

This data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to any external servers.

## Permissions Explained

### "activeTab" Permission
This permission allows Volume Boost Pro to:
- Access the currently active tab when you click the extension icon
- Modify audio elements on the page to adjust volume levels

This permission is only active when you interact with the extension.

### "scripting" Permission
This permission allows Volume Boost Pro to:
- Inject the audio processing script into web pages
- Apply volume amplification to audio and video elements

### "storage" Permission
This permission allows Volume Boost Pro to:
- Save your volume preferences locally
- Remember settings across browser sessions

## Third-Party Services

Volume Boost Pro does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services, analytics platforms, or external servers.

## Audio Processing

All audio amplification is performed locally using the Web Audio API. Audio signals are processed in real-time on your device and are never recorded, stored, or transmitted.

## Data Security

Since we don't collect any data, there is no personal information to secure. Your volume preferences are stored locally on your device using Chrome's secure storage API.

## Children's Privacy

Volume Boost Pro does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 (or applicable age in your jurisdiction). The extension is designed for general audiences and does not include any features that target children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Open Source

Volume Boost Pro is committed to transparency. Our extension code can be inspected by users who want to verify our privacy practices.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the extension's privacy practices, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store support page or by creating an issue on our GitHub repository.

## Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your data. Since Volume Boost Pro does not collect personal data, these rights are inherently respected:

- **Right to Access**: No personal data is stored
- **Right to Deletion**: No personal data exists to delete
- **Right to Portability**: No personal data to transfer
- **Right to Opt-Out**: No tracking or data collection to opt out of

---

**Summary**: Volume Boost Pro is a privacy-respecting extension that processes audio locally, stores preferences on your device only, and never collects, transmits, or shares any personal data.
